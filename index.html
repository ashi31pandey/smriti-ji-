♡ Letter for You ♡

Will you be my Valentine?

To Smriti
 I love you. And with every passing day, this distance feels a little heavier, because missing you has become part of my routine. Still, even across miles, my heart finds its way to you—again and again. Smriti Pandey, daughter of Mr. Himanshu Pandey and Mrs. Deepa Bhainsora Pandey Will you peel that orange for me? And even if you don’t, I promise I always will. I’ll choose you in the smallest moments and the biggest storms, without hesitation. I am endlessly grateful to God for gifting me you. In this world, you are my constant, my comfort, my home Always.
